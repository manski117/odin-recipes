Meat Pasta

This is what spaghetti bolognese looks like

Ingredients needed:

  • 1lb spaghetti
  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1L olive oil
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • Tbsp minced garlic
  • Tbsp fennel seed
  • 1 can parmesean cheese
  • oregano
  • italian seasoning
  • basil leaves
  • 1 onion

Steps

  1. Chop 1 onion into small bits and mince garlic.
  2. Fill bottom of frying pan on low heat with olive oil. Add onioni and garlic to pan and cook until lightly browned.
  3. While onions and garlic are browning, fill a large pot with ~1 gallon of water and place on back burner on high heat to begin boiling
  4. Brown ground beef in frying pan on medium-high heat. Mix with fennel seed to help give it an italian sausage taste. Add salt, pepper, garlic powder if desired.
  5. While meat is browning, add tomato sauce to a second large pan and begin adding herbal ingrediants as well as some parmesian cheese. Optional: use poor a small dash of red wine in for more body and a couple tbsp of sugar to counteract the acidic taste.
  6. Strain meat of greese, then add to sauce pot and cook on medium heat, stirring every 2 minutes
  7. Water should now be boiling. Add noodles to boiling water and stir regularly.
  8. Check noodles until cooked to desired consistency. Strain noodles when done and turn off heat
  9. Add noodles to meat and sauce pot, then stir vigerously to help noodles absorb the flavor. Feel free to add a lot more parmesan cheese at this stage.
